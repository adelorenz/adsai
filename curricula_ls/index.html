Life Sciences and medicine at ADSAI

Synopsis

Background

Life Science, broadly including Medicine and Healthcare, are disciplines that drive conception and development of technologies that, over the last decades, have been impacting society the most. Innovation in these sectors stems from the need to deeply comprehend complex biological phenomena, characterise their fundamental mechanisms and pathological states, learn the effect of our therapeutical interventions onto them and, eventually, enhance our ability to predict human trajectories in both natural and diseases states. The final aim I is always to turn that knowledge into better healthcare, providing more accessible treatment options that eventually increase quality of life.

Motivation

This new concept of modern Life Sciences revolves largely around quantitative modelling and the integration of large-scale heterogenous datasets from DNA/RNA sequencing technologies, imaging capture devices (MRIs, CT scans, PET), digitalised healthcare records etc. Therefore, fundamental actors in this process are Data Scientists that can develop state-of-the-art methodologies to mine high-dimensional noisy heterogenous datasets, supporting technological development and their applications.

ADSAI ambition

During the ADSAI PhD Program, students will be trained to take, in the context of Life Sciences, AI-powered solutions from conceptualisation to completion. A strong focus will be devoted on Machine Learning models for genomics, medical images and electronic health records, with applications to human diseases such as cancer, hearth disease and broad epidemiology. The profiles developed at ADSAI will be able to provide fundamental contributions on the applications of AI to the fields of bioinformatics and biostatistics, focusing on the principles of interpretability, explainability and causality.

Expected outcome

The value of Life Sciences data is far-reaching and constantly evolving, with new companies entering the space across the value chain every year. ADSAI graduates in this track will be ready to apply successfully for lead Data Science positions at healthcare companies, as well as to pursue research careers in both the public and private sectors.
