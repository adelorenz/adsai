Francesco Pauli

Felice Andrea Pellegrino is Associate Professor of Automatica at the Department of Engineering and Architecture, University of Trieste, where he teaches Control Theory and Computer Vision. He has been Research Fellow at SISSA and University of Udine. He is Associate Editor of the IEEE Control Systems Letters and member of the Conference Editorial Boards of the IEEE Control Systems Society and of the European Control Association. His research interests include Control Theory, Machine Learning and Computer Vision. He has co-authored more than 70 peer-reviewed papers and is the inventor of two patents.
