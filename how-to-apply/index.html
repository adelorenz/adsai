Application

Who can apply

Candidates must have acquired a “Laurea magistrale” or equivalent foreign title (giving access to the Doctoral program in the country in which it is issued) by October, 31st 2022

When and how to apply

The application must be submitted through the on line procedure at https://esse3.units.it/Home.do by 13:00 of June, 27th 2022

You will need to include

  1. proof of identity (eg passport)
  2. curriculum vitae et studiorum focusing on experience prior to the PhD and on degree thesis activity (description of techniques acquired, personal contribution, etc)
  3. university qualifications (including transcript of records)
  4. thesis abstract
  5. (optional) two letters of reference from lecturers / researchers (Uploading will be done directly by the lecturer/researcher)

All the documents must be translated in Italian or English if issued in a different language.

For further details see the general call for application and the (specific call for ADSAI)

Evaluation

Applications will be evaluated by a panel of professors/lecturers and experts. The 2022 admission panel is:

  • Dr Ansuini Alessio
  • Dr Bernardini Giulia
  • Prof Bortolussi Luca
  • Dr Caravagna Giulio
  • Prof De Stefano Domenico
  • Dr Manzoni Luca
  • Dr Martellos Stefano

Each applicant is first scored a maximum of 40 points based on academic qualifications. Ranking metrics are:

  • curriculum vitae et studiorum (10 points)
  • exams and thesis (27 points)
  • reference letters (3 points) Applicants are automatically shortlisted and invited for interview (in person or remotely) only if at least 20/40 points are achieved. At the interview, a maximum of other 60 points are assigned, with a minimum of 40/60 points required to be considered eligible for ADSAI recruitment. 2022 interviews: 20-22 July 2022.
