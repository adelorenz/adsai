Admission procedure

2022 application deadline: to be announced soon.

Eligibility

The 2022 call is opened to applicants with any 2nd degree title to be held by October 2022. Each applicant should provide:

  • curriculum vitae et studiorum (free format, reporting exams and marks);
  • final thesis/project abstract;
  • (optional) up to two letters of reference.

Reference letters (english, letter-headed paper) are highly suggested, but not compulsory.

The admission paperwork is managed centrally by the University of Trieste, and general instructions to apply can be found at this webpage.

Panel

Applicants are ranked by a panel of ADSAI faculties. The 2022 admission panel is:

  • Dr Ansuini Alessio
  • Dr Bernardini Giulia
  • Prof Bortolussi Luca
  • Dr Caravagna Giulio
  • Prof De Stefano Domenico
  • Dr Manzoni Luca
  • Dr Martellos Stefano

Ranking

Each applicant is first scored a maximum of 40 points based on academic qualifications. Ranking metrics are:

curriculum vitae et studiorum (10 points)
exams and thesis (27 points)
reference letters (3 points)

Applicants are automatically shortlisted and invited for interview (in person or remotely) only if at least 20/40 points are achieved. At the interview, a maximum of other 60 points are assigned, with a minimum of 40/60 points required to be considered eligible for ADSAI recruitment.
