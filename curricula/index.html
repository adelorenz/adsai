Life sciences and medicine

Modern Life Sciences and Medicine have been revolutionised by the advent of AI technologies that can extract hidden patterns from high-dimensional, noisy datasets. The everyday increasing amount of molecular datasets, electronic health records and healthcare devices provide us with so much information that we can finally use AI to impact our understanding of biological mechanisms at multiple resolutions. This curriculum can train you to apply AI in a variety of settings, from studying microscopic patterns of cellular growth to collective human behaviours, from carachterizing individual diseases such as cancer to population diseases such as COVID-19. With this curriculum you will broadly specialise in the application of AI for precision medicine, building a profile ready to enter the AI-side of the healthcare sector.
