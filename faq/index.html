F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is your teaching in English?
Yes, all of our teaching is in English.
Q: How does the application process work?
Please see the How to Apply page for details.
Q: What about funding?
Students are ranked and, every year, a number of fellowships that cover PhD fees and provide extra salary (around XXXXX €/yr net) are usually available. The number of fellowships might change on a yearly basis, and this type of fellowships are free from a project (i.e., the student can propose a research topic and choose a supervisor). Extra or industrial fellowships can be provided from external funding sources; in that case the research topic and supervisor might be pre-assigned to the fellowship.
Q: I am a self-funded student. Can I apply outside the usual routes?
We do not accept self-funded students.
Q: Are you part of the National PhD in AI?
UNITS is part of the National PhD in AI, and we contribute to its educational offer. We also host students from the programme for their research work.
Q: Do you offer industrial PhD projects?
We do offer industrial PhD projects in collaboration with our industrial partners. Those projects have the same high academic standard as the rest, but typically focus on a specific research question, in close collaboration with the industrial partner. The projects often include an industrial placement with the partner.
