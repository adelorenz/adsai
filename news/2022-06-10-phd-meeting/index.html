ADSAI Open Day

ADSAI 2022 PhD program presentation (June 15, 2022; 14:30 GMT+1).

  • Room Morin, H2bis building, University of Trieste (in person)
  • live streamed online on Microsoft Teams.

The ADSAI Open Day is An opportunity to meet, in person or remotely, the ADSAI faculty. At the Open Day we will present the 2022 program and will be available to discuss application opportunities and the overall ADSAI program structure.

Below, we provide some example videos of ADSAI faculties that discuss research ongoing in their laboratory - more videos might be added before the Open Day. Note that the open day will be recorded and made available at this pag.
