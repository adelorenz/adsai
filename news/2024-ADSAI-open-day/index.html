Embark on Your AI Journey: Discover the ADSAI Open Day 2023!

Are you ready to take the next step in your journey towards becoming a leader in the exciting world of artificial intelligence (AI)? Join us for the ADSAI (Applied Data Science and Artificial Intelligence) Open Day 2023, an event designed specifically for young master's students like you who are eager to explore the endless possibilities of AI research.

Here's why you won't want to miss out:

  • Uncover Cutting-Edge Research: Get a firsthand look at the groundbreaking research happening within our PhD program. From machine learning algorithms to natural language processing techniques, our faculty and students are pushing the boundaries of AI innovation.
  • Meet the Experts: Hear from leading experts in the field as they share insights into their respective curricula:
    • Prof Luca Bortolussi: Dive into the "Industry and Natural Sciences" curriculum and discover how AI is revolutionizing industries ranging from healthcare to finance.
    • Prof Giulia Barbati: Explore the "Life Sciences and Medicine" curriculum and learn about the transformative impact of AI on healthcare and biomedical research.
    • Prof Domenico De Stefano: Delve into the "Economy and Society" curriculum and explore the intersection of AI and social impact, from urban planning to policy-making.
  • Student Success Stories: Wondering what it's really like to pursue a PhD in AI? Hear from our current students as they share their experiences, challenges, and triumphs. From internships at top tech companies to publishing groundbreaking research papers, our students are making waves in the world of AI.
  • Engage and Connect: Connect with like-minded peers and engage with our faculty and students during the interactive Q&A session. Whether you have burning questions about AI ethics or you're curious about the latest research trends, this is your chance to join the conversation and make valuable connections.

Ready to kickstart your AI journey? Mark your calendar for June 6th, 2023, and join us for the ADSAI Open Day 2023. Don't miss this opportunity to immerse yourself in the exciting world of AI research, connect with industry leaders, and take the first step towards shaping the future of technology.

Stay tuned for more details on how to register and participate. We can't wait to welcome you to the ADSAI community and support you on your path to AI greatness!

For more information and updates, visit our website and follow us on social media. See you at the ADSAI Open Day 2023!
